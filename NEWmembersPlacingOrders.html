Customizing Your Order:
  • The default box for each week will be pre-loaded into your shopping cart, but you can change your order from Monday 5pm - Wednesday 11:59pm.
  • To change what you'll get in your box, simply take anything you don't want out of the shopping cart and replace it with items you prefer from anything in the store.
  • Most of the time you can order multiple of one item, but at times we have to limit the per-member number of an item to make sure we can spread them out to everyone.
  • YOU MUST click the "proceed" or "checkout" button on the cart to finalize any changes to your order.
  • If you're not sure you've done it correctly, feel free to shoot us an email and we can confirm if it's gone through or not.

Extra Produce
  • You can order EXTRA PRODUCE any week of the CSA.
  • All you have to do is add any extra items above the base amount for that week (usually 6-7 items) to your order.
    • Any payment required for the extra produce will be reflected in your cart.
    • YOU MUST click the "proceed" or "checkout" button on the cart to finalize any changes to your order.
    • You'll be charged for the extra produce on CSA pickup days ([Pickup Time]).
  • If you have any problems adding extra items to your cart or can't check out with those items, let us know; it's usually just a matter of getting your payment method set up, and we're happy to help.

Bulk Items and Meat
  • Along with the regular vegetable items, we also have Bulk Vegetables, and sometimes we have Chicken and Beef for sale to CSA members.

    You can find these items...
    • Using the tabs on the left side of the screen (computer)
    • From the dropdown menu at the top of the screen (phone)
  • YOU MUST click the "proceed" or "checkout" button on the cart to finalize any changes to your order.
  • Meat and bulk vegetable orders are not part of the CSA "box" and will be paid for on day of CSA pickup (Friday).

Delivery Holds
  • If you're going to miss any weeks and don't want the box packed for you that week:

    1. Click on the "Delivery Hold" tab from the main page of your account ([Link to Account])
    2. Fill in any dates you'll be gone and don't want the box packed for you.

    This will let us know you're away, and will allow your account to get refunded for the value of that box.
  • However, if you can't make a pickup on Friday, but STILL WANT THE BOX, just let us know that week, and we can work out a way to get your box to you on another day.
We hope all this helps you get going successfully with our CSA.

If you ever have any questions, concerns or difficulties, please let us know. We're always happy to help as best we can.
