Customizing your Weekly Orders
  • Each Monday at 5pm, our online store will open. You can access it here: [Link to Web Store]
  • When you go into the online store, the default box for the week will be pre-loaded into your shopping cart.
  • To change what you'll get in your box, simply remove anything you don't want from the shopping cart and replace it with items that you prefer.
  • If there's an item you REALLY like, most of the time you can order multiple, but there are occasionally times when we have to limit the per-member number of an item to ensure that we can spread them out to everyone.
  • VERY IMPORTANT: You must click the "proceed" or "checkout" button on the cart to finalize any changes to your order. Otherwise, your changes won't go through and you'll get the default items.
  • If you're not sure you've done it correctly, feel free to shoot us an email. We can confirm if it's gone through.
  • The store will close Wednesday night at 11:59pm. At that point, you won't be able to make adjustments to your order for the week. This gives us time to harvest, wash, and pack the correct amounts of delicious veggies for everyone.
Extra Produce
  • You can order EXTRA PRODUCE any week of the CSA. Simply add any extra items above the base amount for that week.
  • Any additional charges for the extra produce will be reflected in your cart and you will be charged on CSA pickup days ([Pickup Time]).
  • If you have any problems adding extra items to your cart or can't check out with those items, let us know. We're happy to resolve any technical issues in getting payment methods set up.

Bulk Items and Meat
  • Along with the regular vegetable items, we also have Bulk Vegetables, Chicken and Beef for sale to CSA members.
  • Here's where you can find these items:
    • On Computers: Use the tabs on the left side of the screen.
    • On Mobile Devices: Use the dropdown menu at the top of the screen.
  • Meat and Bulk Vegetable orders aren't part of the CSA box (they cost extra), and will be paid for on the day of CSA pickup.

  • At your chosen pickup location, there will be a box with your name on it, and if you've ordered any extra meat items, there will also be a cooler with your name on it.
  • We ask that you please bring your own bags, transfer your items into your bags, and leave the boxes/coolers at your pickup location so that we can re-use them the following week.
  • However, if you've forgotten your bags, you are welcome to take the box home with you, but we ask that you please remember to bring it back the following week.
  • No need to check in with anyone; you just collect your order and carry on with the rest of your day!

Delivery Holds
  • If you're going to be out of town and don't want the box packed for you that week, click on the "Delivery Hold" tab from the main page of your account and fill in any dates you will be gone.
  • You can access your account here: [Link to Account]
  • Placing a Delivery Hold will allow your account to get refunded for the value of that box.
  • However, if you can't make a pickup on Friday, but STILL WANT THE BOX, just let us know that week and we can work out a way to get your box to you on another day.

Account Balance / Refunds
  • If you use any delivery holds, or if we mess something up, the system allows your account to be credited for the appropriate amount.
  • This refund stays with your account and can be issued by us as a refund at the end of the season, or it can be used against the cost of any future membership.
  • PLEASE NOTE: In the past, we've allowed this balance to purchase Extra Produce or other items, but that has been a logistical challenge and we are trying to move away from that option. If this is a specific desire of yours, feel free to contact us and we can work out the details.

CSA Newsletter
  • We publish a newsletter each week for our members, letting you know about what's happening on the farm, what's going to be in your default box, and any issues that might affect your weekly order.
  • We'll include a link to the latest newletter in the weekly email (which comes on Mondays).
  • If you want to look through our past newsletters, they're available on our website: FullCircleFarmMi.com
