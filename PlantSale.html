Spring Plant Sale

We've decided that farming is just too much fun to keep it all to ourselves, so we're inviting you to join in on the merriment.

Introducing our First Annual Plant Sale at Full Circle Farm!

What Are We Selling?
All sorts of things! But here's a short list:
  • Cucumbers
  • Flowers
  • Herbs
  • Hot Peppers
  • Sweet Peppers
  • Tomatoes
Why on Earth Would We Sell Plants?
  • We believe that everyone should experience the joy of growing.
  • Growing your own food helps you appreciate it more (and when it doesn't go well, it makes you appreciate farmers!)
  • Growing your own flowers ensures that pollinators have food that is free of harmful chemicals.
Are These Plants Organic?
  • While we are not USDA Organic certified, we do stick to organic practices on our farm.
  • That means:
    • No Pesticides
    • No Herbicides
    • No Fungicides
    • No Industrially-Created Fertilizer; we make our own compost!
  • Some of the seeds we ordered didn't come from organic plants, but we've identified the organic seeds with an (Organic) in the title

How Does it Work?
  • Visit our Spring Plant Sale Page: FullCircleFarmMi.com/s/Order
  • Choose your plants.
    • Click on a plant to get more information.
    • Choose Quantity.
    • Click Add to order.
    • Click on the Shopping Cart icon at the top of the screen to begin the checkout process.
  • We'll nurture your tiny little seeds until they become seedlings.
  • Then we'll deliver them to your chosen pickup location on Friday, May 19th.

    You can pick them up between 4:30pm - 8pm.


Questions? Concerns? Please let us know. We're always happy to help as best we can.
